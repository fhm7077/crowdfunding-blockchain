    About the Platform

    Crowdfunding is the technique of raising small amounts of money from a large number of individuals, usually over the Internet, to fund a project or endeavor. This crowdfunding application is like no other as it does not transfer the amount which is funded directly to the reciever, rather the amount is stored in a smart contract.
